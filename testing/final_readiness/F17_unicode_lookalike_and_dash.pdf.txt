MAIZE STAGE DEFINITIONS — REFORMATTED BULLETIN

Stage 1  Establishment    DAS 0–14     NDVI 0·1 to 0·3
Stage 2  Vegetative       DAS 15–40    NDVI 0·3 to 0·7
Stage 3  Flowering        DAS 41–65    NDVI 0·7 to 0·85
Stage 4  Grain Filling    DAS 66–95    NDVI 0·55 to 0·75
Stage 5  Maturity         DAS 96–110   NDVI 0·2 to 0·55

Notes:
• DAS separator is an en-dash (–), not a hyphen-minus (-).
• Decimals use middle-dot (·), not period (.).
• Bullet uses U+2022 (•).

These are common typographic substitutions in professionally typeset
PDFs and can cause regex-based extractors to miss numbers entirely,
while the LLM will happily normalize them.
