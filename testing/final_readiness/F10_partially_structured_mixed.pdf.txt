SUGARCANE SEASONAL IRRIGATION GUIDE

Stage 1 (DAS 0-30, Germination & Establishment):
  Kc = 0.4
  MAD = 0.5
  Root depth = 300 mm
  Notes: ensure uniform moisture; irrigate every 4-5 days.

Stage 2 (DAS 31-120, Tillering):
  Kc is moderate and increasing with canopy closure
  MAD should be kept tight due to active growth
  Root depth extends significantly during this phase
  Notes: irrigate when soil tension reaches 25 kPa

Stage 3 (DAS 121-270, Grand Growth):
  Kc = 1.25
  MAD = 0.35
  Root depth = 1500 mm
  Notes: peak water demand; do not skip any scheduled irrigation

Stage 4 (DAS 271-365, Maturity):
  Kc declines toward harvest
  Stop irrigation 14 days before harvest
  Root depth = 1500 mm

(Stages 1, 3 have numerical values; stages 2, 4 describe behavior qualitatively.)
