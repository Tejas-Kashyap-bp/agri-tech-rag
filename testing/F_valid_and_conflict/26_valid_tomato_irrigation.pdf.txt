TOMATO IRRIGATION PARAMETERS — DRIP SYSTEM
Horticulture Extension Bulletin 2025/TOM/03
Crop: Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)
Document Type: Irrigation Parameters

Table 1 — Stage-wise Irrigation Parameters

Stage Code | Stage Name     | Kc   | MAD  | Root Depth (mm)
-----------|----------------|------|------|-----------------
S1         | Transplant     | 0.50 | 0.40 | 300
S2         | Vegetative     | 0.85 | 0.35 | 500
S3         | Flowering      | 1.10 | 0.30 | 700
S4         | Fruit Set      | 1.15 | 0.30 | 800
S5         | Harvest        | 0.80 | 0.40 | 800

Stage-wise irrigation notes:
S1 (Transplant): Light, frequent irrigation (5-8 mm/day) for the first 10 days.
S2 (Vegetative): Irrigate when 35% of available soil water is depleted.
S3 (Flowering): Critical period. Do NOT allow water stress.
S4 (Fruit Set): Maintain steady moisture to avoid blossom-end rot and cracking.
S5 (Harvest): Reduce to 60% of peak irrigation. Stop 5 days before final pick.

General rules:
- All Kc values reference FAO-56 Table 12.
- MAD adjustments: sandy soils -0.05, clay soils +0.05.
- Drip preferred; flood irrigation not recommended for this crop.
