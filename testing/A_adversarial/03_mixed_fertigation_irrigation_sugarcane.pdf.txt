SUGARCANE COMBINED IRRIGATION + FERTIGATION PLAN
Farm: Plot 14-B, Belgaum Region
Prepared: Sept 2025

------------------------------------------------------
Week 1-2 (after planting):
  - Irrigation: 25 mm every 4 days via drip
  - Fertigation: 15 kg/ha urea dissolved in irrigation water
  - Root zone: 30 cm

Week 3-6 (tillering):
  - Irrigation scheduled when soil tension reaches 30 kPa
  - Fertigation: weekly N-P-K at 19-19-19 @ 8 kg/ha
  - Combine irrigation and nutrient delivery; do NOT irrigate plain water
    during this window.

Week 7-20 (grand growth):
  - Kc ~ 1.25 (reference FAO-56)
  - Daily drip irrigation preferred; fertigation split across every second
    irrigation event.
  - N: 120 kg/ha split across this window
  - K: 80 kg/ha

Week 21-40 (maturity):
  - Reduce irrigation frequency
  - Stop fertigation by week 32
  - Last irrigation 14 days before harvest

------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
This document intentionally combines irrigation scheduling and fertigation
because on drip systems they are operationally inseparable. Classifying
this as purely "irrigation" or purely "fertigation" will lose information.
