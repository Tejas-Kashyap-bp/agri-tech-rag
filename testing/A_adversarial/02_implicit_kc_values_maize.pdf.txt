MAIZE CROP COEFFICIENT NOTES (Kc)

Table 1 — Kc Profile

Stage          | Relative Kc
---------------|------------
Establishment  | low
Vegetative     | rising
Flowering      | peak
Grain filling  | slightly below peak
Maturity       | declining to minimal

Interpretation guidelines:
- "low" = baseline water demand
- "peak" = maximum seasonal demand
- Use the shape of the curve rather than exact numbers; the actual Kc should
  be tuned to local ETo and soil type.

Note: no numerical Kc values are published in this bulletin because
research across agro-climatic zones has shown wide variation. Users should
consult FAO-56 for reference Kc.

— AgriTech Research Cell, Bulletin 14
