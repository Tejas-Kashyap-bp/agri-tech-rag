WHEAT IRRIGATION ADVISORY — RABI SEASON
Department of Agronomy, District Extension Office
Issued: October 2025

General Guidance for Wheat Growers
-----------------------------------

Wheat is a water-sensitive crop and requires careful irrigation management.
Farmers should apply MODERATE irrigation during the early vegetative phase and
ensure ADEQUATE moisture during tillering. The crop responds well to TIMELY
watering around the crown root initiation stage.

During booting and heading, water demand is HIGH. A LIGHT irrigation may be
beneficial after flowering depending on weather. Avoid EXCESSIVE irrigation
during maturity as this may encourage lodging.

Kc values are generally LOW in the beginning, INCREASE during peak vegetative
growth, and TAPER OFF near harvest.

Soil moisture should be maintained at a SATISFACTORY level throughout the
season. Use your judgement based on soil feel and plant appearance.
