COMMON IRRIGATION SCHEDULE — KHARIF CEREALS
State Horticulture & Agronomy Board

This schedule applies to commonly grown kharif cereals in the region.
Farmers may adapt the schedule based on local conditions and specific
crop planted.

Week 1-2:   2 irrigations of 25 mm each
Week 3-6:   1 irrigation per 5 days, 35 mm each
Week 7-10:  1 irrigation per 4 days, 40 mm each (peak water demand)
Week 11-14: 1 irrigation per 7 days, 30 mm each
Week 15+:   Stop irrigation 10 days before harvest

Applicable to: all cereal crops in the kharif rotation including but not
limited to coarse grains and staple cereals.

Note: This schedule is intentionally crop-agnostic. See crop-specific
bulletins for Kc values and stage-wise refinements.
