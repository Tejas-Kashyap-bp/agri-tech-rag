COMPARATIVE FERTIGATION GUIDE — CEREAL CROPS
Punjab Agricultural University, 2025

This bulletin covers BOTH maize and wheat fertigation programs because
farmers in this zone typically rotate the two crops and use the same
drip-fertigation infrastructure.

MAIZE (Kharif)
---------------
Stage 1 (0-14 DAS): N 20 kg/ha, applied via fertigation in 3 splits
Stage 2 (15-40 DAS): N 60 kg/ha, K 30 kg/ha
Stage 3 (41-65 DAS): N 40 kg/ha, K 40 kg/ha, P 20 kg/ha
Stage 4 (66-95 DAS): K 30 kg/ha only

WHEAT (Rabi)
-------------
Stage 1 (0-21 DAS): N 25 kg/ha in 2 splits during CRI
Stage 2 (22-55 DAS): N 50 kg/ha during tillering
Stage 3 (56-90 DAS): N 30 kg/ha, K 20 kg/ha during booting
Stage 4 (91-140 DAS): none

Common recommendations for BOTH crops:
- Use water-soluble fertilizers
- Avoid fertigation in the 10 days preceding harvest
- Flush drip lines with plain water after every fertigation event
